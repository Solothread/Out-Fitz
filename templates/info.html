About Out-Fitz


Out-Fitz will help you diversify your wardrobe and style, never question if you have already worn an outfit again!

With Out-Fitz you can upload a picture of your outfit daily along with a date & time tag and a description of what you wore to keep track of what you wear every day.

When you are deciding what to wear you can scroll through all of your previous outfits to ensure that you do not repeat!

Byte Size: Adam, Isaac and Nikki, created the application with the intent of eliminating the factor of forgetfulness when picking what to wear
